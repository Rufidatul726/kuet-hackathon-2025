Recipe Name: Chocolate Cake
Ingredients:
- 200g Sugar
- 300g Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 100g Cocoa Powder
- 1 tsp Baking Powder

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Mix the dry ingredients together (flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder).
3. Add eggs and mix until smooth.
4. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 30 minutes.

Tags: Sweet, Dessert, Cake

Recipe Name: Pancakes
Ingredients:
- 1 cup Flour
- 1 tbsp Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 1 cup Milk
- 1 tsp Baking Powder

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients.
2. Add the eggs and milk, and whisk until smooth.
3. Heat a non-stick skillet and cook pancakes for about 2 minutes on each side.

Tags: Breakfast, Sweet, Quick
